Services 

One of the Big Four mobile network providers in the UK, EE offers a variety of SIM and contract solutions for mobile phones.
EE offers a variety of plans, including Sim-only and handset-supplied choices.

Additionally, families can use EE's shared plans, which let them add up to five additional devices to a single inclusive package. Additionally, there is an up to 20% discount available.
EE has a variety of incentives, but the customers we polled said they weren't happy with what was on offer. You can enjoy one of a number of Smart Benefits if you sign up for a Full Works or Smart plan for your phone or SIM.
 

One of the Big Four mobile service providers, O2, offers a variety of Sim packages.
O2 offers a large selection of pay monthly and PAYG handsets, from low-end steals to the most expensive models. You can select from well-known brands like Apple, Huawei, and Samsung as well as well-liked niche brands like Xiaomi, OnePlus, and Doro. The ability to pre-order new flagship phones is typically available.
O2 also offers less expensive 'Like New' handsets on pay-monthly plans, allowing customers who are unable to afford the exorbitant price tags for a brand-new cellphone to purchase a used phone that has been thoroughly inspected and securely erased.
 

 

A variety of Sim-only and mobile phone contract offers are available from BT Mobile, a mobile service provider. Because BT Mobile is a virtual provider, it makes use of the network infrastructure of another provider, in this case the EE network. It provides coverage for 3G, 4G, and 5G. Although it does offer a limited selection of smartphone contracts, BT Mobile primarily offers Sim-only plans that you use with your own cellphone.

Additionally, you can pay more to get faster 4G download speeds (up to 60Mbps instead of 30Mbps), but this is now included for free with any 10GB plan or higher. You can also add bundles of extra data or minutes.
