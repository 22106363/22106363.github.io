Samsung

Samsung's new flagship S23 Ultra constantly impresses but rarely shocks, like many modern smartphones do. After using the device for a few weeks, we are torn between excitement and déjà vu. It is large, powerful, and exquisitely built.

In the end, how much it impresses you will depend on how well you know the prior model. It's one of the best Android phones money can buy, but anyone carrying an S22 Ultra should pass.
